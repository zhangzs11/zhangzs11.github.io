Monster Chase Game

For my final project, I intend to create a simple monster chase game. The core gameplay will involve a monster AI that attempts to approach and catch the player. The player must navigate a scene filled with obstacles to evade the monster. If the monster catches the player, the game will display a full-screen scare effect.

Game Features:

  • Monster AI: The monster will have a basic AI system that enables it to chase the player. It will navigate the scene, avoiding obstacles to pursue the player.
  • Obstacles: The scene will include various static obstacles that neither the monster nor the player can pass through.
  • Scare Effect: When the monster catches the player, the game will trigger a full-screen scare effect to simulate a “jumpscare” moment, enhancing the horror experience.
  • Audio Effects: Audio will play a significant role in building tension and immersing the player in the game’s atmosphere.

Engine Systems Used:

Graphics System:

  • The graphics system will render the scene, including the player, monster, and obstacles.
  • It will also handle the scare effect when the monster catches the player, ensuring a smooth full-screen visual.

Audio System:

  • The audio system will provide sound effects for the monster.
  • The monster’s sound will dynamically change based on its distance to the player, increasing tension as the monster approaches.
  • Additional sound effects will trigger during key events, such as when the monster catches the player, to heighten the scare factor.
